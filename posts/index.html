Metaphor_visuals_pilot

This post is the first entry in a diary of my trials and errors in creating visualizations for my doctoral thesis. In summary: I have an excel table with several thousands of figurative language examples in Latvian, which are coded with 0 and 1 values that correspond with specific metaphor categories. I’m working on visualizations in order to make all the data simple to percieve, available to others, and - hopefully in the future - interactive.

The first step was about scraping all Riga archives to get my hands on every possible edition of the 24 piano preludes by the composer Jānis Medinš.
